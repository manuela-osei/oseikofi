THIS WEBSITE WAS MADE FOR A SCHOOL ASSIGNMENT, WHICH REQUIRED THE MAKING OF A WEBSITE WITH HTML, CSS, JAVASCRIPT AND JQUERY TO TELL A PERSONAL STORY OR TO MAKE A PORTFOLIO. THE AUTHOR DECIDED TO TELL A PERSONAL STORY. THE STORY BEEN TOLD IS THE LIFE STORY OF THE AUTHORS'S FATHER OR BETTER SAID HIS BIOGRAPHY. IT IS A STORY THAT HAS BEEN VERY PERSONAL AND MEANINGFUL TO THE FAMILY. THE FATHER OF THE AUTHOR IS CONSIDERED TO BE A ROLE MODEL TO ALL THIS OFFSPRING AND THEREFORE AN IMPORTANT FIGURE IN THE FAMILY. IT IS THEREFORE IMPORTANT TO WRITE DOWN OF SHARE HIS STORY.
